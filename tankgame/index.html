Press enter to join (twice for two keyboard players), or plug in a game controller.
Controls:
  • KB1: wasd (move), c (shoot), v (reload)
  • KB2: arrows (move), / (shoot), . (reload)
  • Joypad: analog sticks (move), RB (shoot), LB (reload)
Recommended browser: Chromium (Firefox lags).
